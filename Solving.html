Problem Solving


It is the ability to identify challenges, analyze possible solutions, and implement the best course of action to resolve an issue efficiently.

It is a critical skill in both personal and professional settings, helping individuals navigate complex situations and make informed decisions.


  • Identifying the Problem
    • Understanding the root cause of an issue rather than just the symptoms.
    • Gathering relevant information and analyzing the situation.
    • Asking the right questions to define the problem clearly.

  • Analyzing Possible Solutions
    • Breaking down the problem into smaller, manageable parts.
    • Brainstorming multiple solutions and considering alternatives.
    • Evaluating the pros and cons of each approach.

  • Decision-Making
    • Choosing the best solution based on logic, facts, and available resources.
    • Considering short-term and long-term consequences.
    • Using techniques like the SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) to assess options.

  • Implementing the Solution
    • Developing an action plan with clear steps.
    • Assigning responsibilities if teamwork is involved.
    • Executing the solution efficiently while monitoring progress.

  • Evaluating the Outcome
    • Reviewing results to see if the problem was effectively solved.
    • Identifying lessons learned for future problem-solving situations.
    • Making adjustments or improvements if needed.

  • Critical Thinking & Creativity
    • Thinking outside the box to find innovative solutions.
    • Using logical reasoning and analytical skills.
    • Applying lateral thinking to approach problems from different angles.

  • Adaptability & Resilience
    • Staying calm under pressure and handling challenges with confidence.
    • Being open to feedback and adjusting strategies when needed.
    • Learning from failures and improving problem-solving approaches.

  • Communication & Collaboration
    • Clearly explaining problems and proposed solutions to others.
    • Working with teammates to brainstorm and implement fixes.
    • Actively listening to different perspectives to refine the solution.
